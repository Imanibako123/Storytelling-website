LION AND THE MOUSE

Once upon a time in a vast jungle, a mighty lion was taking a nap under a shady tree. As he slept, a little mouse scurried by and accidentally ran over the lion's paw. Startled, the lion woke up with a roar and caught the tiny mouse in his massive paw .
